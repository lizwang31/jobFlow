How RAG Works
After you upload a resume, it is split into chunks and embedded into Pinecone.
Each time you click the "AI Analyze" button on a job card, the system will:

1. Embed the job description
2. Retrieve the most relevant resume snippets
3. Call the LLM with JD + resume context
4. Generate a match score, cover letter, and interview questions
